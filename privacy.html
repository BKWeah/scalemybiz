Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

ScaleMyBiz™ (operated at scalemybiz.global) is a digital business planning tool. For privacy enquiries, contact us at hello@scalemybiz.global.

When you use ScaleMyBiz™, we may collect:

4. Email Communications

After purchase, you will receive a confirmation email with access to your plan. We may occasionally send product updates. You can unsubscribe at any time by replying to any email with "Unsubscribe."

Your data is processed via EmailJS (email delivery) and Selar (payments). Both services maintain their own privacy and security standards. We take reasonable measures to protect information submitted through this site.

This site uses minimal cookies for session management and basic analytics. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. Contact us at hello@scalemybiz.global to exercise these rights.
